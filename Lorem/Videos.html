Hagfish sliming video

Shown in this video is a Hagfish that is agitated with a small electric shock so that it excretes slime. The slime is then collected and shown to the camera.

Anti-shark slime weapon

Recording of Hagfish succesfully veering off predators with its slime. The thick slime of the Hagfish clogs the mouth and gills of its predators making it near impossible to get eaten.
